John Coates London–Paris Number Theory Seminar

Geometric Perspectives on the Langlands Programme

Dates: 16–17 June 2026  |  Location: Queen Mary University of London

The 39th meeting of the John Coates London–Paris Number Theory Seminar will take place 16–17 June 2026 at Queen Mary University of London. The meeting will explore recent advances around geometric perspectives on the Langlands programme.

Invited Speakers

Lambert A'Campo IHES
Robin Bartlett Queen Mary University of London

Jean-François Dat Sorbonne Université (IMJ-PRG)

Miriam Norris Queen Mary University of London
Sandra Rozensztajn ENS de Lyon
Rong Zhou University of Cambridge

The John Coates London-Paris Number Theory Seminar is a biannual event, meeting in London in the Spring and in Paris in the Autumn. Each meeting is typically spread over a Monday afternoon and Tuesday morning, and is devoted to a particular theme related to number theory in a broad sense. The seminar was renamed in the honour of John Coates (26/1/1945 - 9/5/2022) in 2023, in recognition of the crucial role he played in the founding of the seminar in 2006.
